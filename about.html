Hey there! I’m Aruzhan — a high school student who’s passionate about how the world works and dreams of becoming an engineer.

I built this website from scratch using HTML and PHP, combining my love for coding, design, and engineering into one creative space. It started as a simple idea — but it quickly grew into something much more meaningful. Now, it’s my way of showing how exciting, diverse, and full of possibility engineering truly is. Engineering isn’t just one thing. There are so many branches like aerospace, biomedical, mechanical, civil, nuclear, and more and each one has the power to change lives. I wanted to create a place where students (especially those who are just starting to explore) can learn what engineering really means and discover which type speaks to them. As a girl in STEM, I also hope to inspire more girls to believe in themselves and their ability to solve problems, build things, and shape the future. We need more voices, more creativity, and more courage in this field — and that starts by showing what’s possible. This site is just the beginning. I plan to add real-world engineering projects, fun explanations, videos, and more. Thanks so much for being here. I hope this space sparks your curiosity and maybe even your own path into engineering.
